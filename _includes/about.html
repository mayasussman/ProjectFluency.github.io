The Problem

There are 23 million adults in the United States with limited English proficiency. For them, language limits day-to-day life, access to jobs they are otherwise skilled for, even the ability to help their kids succeed in school.

While adult English learners frequently encounter English speakers in their day-to-day lives, they often lack the confidence to practice speaking English outside a classroom setting.

Our Mission

Project Fluency helps adult English learners become confident English speakers.

We help learners increase their confidence speaking English by providing practical content for everyday conversations, and structuring daily speaking practice into their lives.

The Solution

We partner with ESL classes and English conversation clubs to deliver practical conversational content, augmented by daily nudges to help learners build a habit of practicing English in their everyday lives. The result is a positive feedback loop that improves both confidence and English proficiency over time.
